The Story

Started in 2018 by Josh and Leah Hazzard in Milwaukee WI in the neighborhood of Kops Park.

Hazzard Brewing CO. is a brewery owned by the community.

After 10k signatures, Josh decided this actually was a feasible idea and now fast forward three years and Hazzard Brewing CO. is a 12 tap brewery with six staples and six seasonal/rotating taps.

Come on into our tap room and try one of our beers, your second beer is always on us!
